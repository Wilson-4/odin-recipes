Burger

Description

Learn how to cook steak burgers on the grill with ground beef and steak seasoning. The Montreal seasoning adds real steak flavor â€” you may not realize the burgers are just made with ground beef!

ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 teaspoon Montreal steak seasoning
  • 4 hamburger buns, split

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate.

  2. Step 2

    Mix ground beef and steak seasoning together in a bowl. Form mixture into four patties.

  3. step 3

    Grill patties on the preheated grill until no longer pink in the center, 5 to 6 minutes per side. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).

  4. step 4

    Serve on hamburger buns.
